POSITIONING RATIONALE: Austin’s STR market is large (17,448 listings) and moderately seasonal, with occupancy 54% and ADR $262.9 in the latest AirDNA snapshot [S5]. Competing as budget stock in this environment is fragile because supply grew and many listings already compete on discount [S5]. Premium-lite is more defensible for an entire-home SFR: quality + reliability + event-aware pricing.

Regulatory structure now allows STRs as accessory use across residential zones with active licensing, but also introduces stricter platform compliance beginning July 1, 2026 and ongoing HOT reporting requirements [S1]. That favors disciplined operators who can maintain licensing, reporting, and response standards.

Demand catalysts remain durable: SXSW (Mar 12-18, 2026), MotoGP at COTA (Mar 27-29, 2026), ACL Fest weekends (Oct 2-4 and Oct 9-11, 2026), F1 USGP (Oct 23-25, 2026), and UT home football weekends [S8][S13][S14][S15][S16]. This supports a premium-lite strategy with event-based compression pricing, not year-round luxury positioning.

5. POSITIONING CONTROL RULES

5.1 Strategic Guardrails

RULE 1: Licensing Integrity

  • Rule: No listing may go live without active Austin STR license and current renewal date controls.
  • Rationale: Platform delisting risk and enforcement from July 1, 2026 [S1].

RULE 2: Tax Reporting Discipline

  • Rule: Quarterly HOT filings always submitted, including zero reports.
  • Rationale: City reporting requirement even when platforms remit [S1].

RULE 3: Response-Time SLA

  • Rule: First guest message response <=60 minutes; emergency local response ability <=2 hours.
  • Rationale: Aligns with city local-contact expectations and review protection [S1].

RULE 4: Rate Floor Protection

  • Rule: Never discount below modeled variable-cost plus positioning floor.
  • Rationale: Avoids low-value guest mix and margin erosion.

RULE 5: Event Pricing Protocol

  • Rule: Event windows pre-loaded 90-180 days out; minimum-stay controls applied.
  • Rationale: Capture compression demand windows [S8][S13][S14][S15][S16].
