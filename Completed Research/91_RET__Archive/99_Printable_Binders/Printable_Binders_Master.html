• Step 1: Locate Main Water Shut-off on the exterior side wall (left of the garage). Look for the blue painted valve.
• Step 2: Turn the valve perpendicular (90 degrees) to the pipe.
• Step 3: Place towels to contain pooling water. Do not use wet/dry vac if electrical outlets are submerged.
• Step 4: Call Flush-It-Right Plumbing: (555) 123-4567.

2. POWER OUTAGE & ELECTRICAL

• Step 1: Check if neighbors have power (is it a grid outage?).
• Step 2: If isolated, locate the Main Electrical Panel on the exterior wall (right of the garage).
• Step 3: Identify any tripped breakers (switches flipped to the middle). Push fully to OFF, then back to ON.
• Step 4: Deploy emergency flashlights from the Pantry and Primary Closet.
• Step 5: Call Austin CoolAir & Electric: (555) 987-6543.

• Step 1: Check the Nest Thermostat in the 158" Hallway for an "E" error code.
• Step 2: Check the HVAC breaker in the main electrical panel.
• Step 3: Deploy backup portable fans stored in the Primary Walk-In Closet.
• Step 4: Call Austin CoolAir & Electric for same-day emergency dispatch.

4. SMART LOCK FAILURE / LOCKOUT

• Step 1: Verify the guest is using the correct 4-digit code.
• Step 2: Direct the guest to the physical lockbox hidden on the right side of the patio.

[CRITICAL] Speed and empathy define the guest's perception of failure. Acknowledge within 15 minutes.

• Script: "Hi [Name], I'm so sorry you're dealing with [Issue]. I'm looking into this right now and will update you in exactly [Timeframe, e.g., 15 mins]."

STEP 2: TRIAGE & SEVERITY ASSESSMENT

• Severity 1 (Minor Annoyance): e.g., Wi-Fi slow, missing spoon.

- Resolution: Send instructions or offer a drop-off during the next day.

• Severity 2 (Functional Issue): e.g., Coffee maker broken, TV won't turn on.

- Resolution: Dispatch Handyman within 12 hours. Offer minor comp if requested.

• Severity 3 (Habitability Impact): e.g., HVAC broken in summer, no hot water.

- Resolution: Dispatch Emergency Vendor immediately. Inform guest of ETA.

• Severity 4 (Life/Safety/Damage): e.g., Flood, Fire, Lockout.

- Resolution: Use Emergency Procedures Guide. Secure guest safety first.

STEP 3: VENDOR DISPATCH

• Action: Contact appropriate vendor from the Vendor Directory.
• Requirement: Demand a specific ETA window. Relay this window to the guest.

STEP 4: GUEST FOLLOW-UP

• Action: Message guest 1 hour after vendor ETA to confirm resolution.
• Script: "Hi [Name], our tech marked this as complete. Is everything working perfectly for you now?"

STEP 5: SERVICE RECOVERY (COMPENSATION)

• Minor Issue: No comp required if fixed quickly. Offer late checkout.
• Moderate Issue (Loss of amenity for 1 day): $25-$50 refund or cover a local meal.
• Major Issue (Loss of habitability): Refund the affected night(s) + assist in relocation if unfixable.

STEP 6: LOG & PREVENT

• Action: Record the issue in `MAINTENANCE_LOG.csv`.
• Action: Trigger the Performance Feedback Loop to ensure the issue is engineered out of the system.
